# Privacy Policy - SkyBeast Ascension

Last updated: February 15, 2026

SkyBeast Ascension ("we", "our", "us") provides an online multiplayer game experience. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:
- Account data: player name, email address
- Authentication data: login provider information (for example Google sign-in)
- Gameplay data: tier, XP, score, quests, settings, and progression state
- Technical data: basic request logs and session information

## How We Use Information

We use collected data to:
- Create and manage player accounts
- Save and restore player progress
- Display leaderboards and multiplayer presence
- Secure sessions and prevent abuse
- Improve game stability and gameplay quality

## Cookies and Session Data

We use HTTP-only session cookies to keep players signed in securely. These cookies are used for authentication and account continuity.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.  
We do not share personal information with third parties except when required to operate core services (for example hosting/auth providers) or to comply with legal obligations.

## Data Retention

Account and gameplay data are retained while your account is active or as needed to provide services and meet legal requirements.

## Security

We apply reasonable technical safeguards, including secure session handling and transport protections on production services.

## Children

SkyBeast Ascension is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable laws in your region.

## Your Choices

You may request account updates or deletion by contacting support.
